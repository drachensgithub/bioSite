Who Is Nicole Dale?

Nicole Dale was Born is Kansas City, Kansas (as seen in the first picture) in 1966. She works at PNC Bank (shown in the second picture) and she loves cooking and eating food (shown in the third picture). She tries to visit her hometown whenever possible and enjoys rooting for the Kansas City Chiefs. She has been with PNC Bank for almost 11 years now and still loves it. Her favorite food is sushi and anything German.
